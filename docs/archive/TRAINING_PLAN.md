# NCR-CAPA Management System - Training Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the training strategy to ensure successful adoption of the new NCR-CAPA Management System across the organization.

## 1. Objectives
- Ensure all users can log in and navigate the system.
- Train users on how to report NCRs correctly.
- Train Managers on approval workflows.
- Train PICs on CAPA execution and reporting.
- Train Admins on system maintenance.

## 2. Target Audience
1. **General Staff**: Initiators of NCRs.
2. **Department Managers**: Approvers and Reviewers.
3. **QC Department**: Verifiers and Closers.
4. **IT Administrators**: System maintainers.

## 4. Resources Required
- Projector & Screen.
- Laptop/PC for each participant (or bring your own).
- Test Environment (Staging Server) access.
- Printed Quick Reference Cards.
- Feedback Forms.

## 5. Training Content
1. **Introduction** (10 mins): Why we are changing systems.
2. **System Demo** (20 mins): Live walkthrough.
3. **Hands-on Practice** (60 mins): Participants create test NCRs.
4. **Q&A** (15 mins): Addressing concerns.
5. **Quiz** (15 mins): Simple check of understanding.

## 6. Post-Training Support
- **Helpdesk**: Dedicated email `support@ncr-system.com`.
- **Walk-in Support**: IT Dept open for questions 14:00-16:00 daily for 1 week.
- **Refresher Session**: Scheduled 1 month after Go-Live.
