# Quick Reference Card

## 1. NCR Process at a Glance
1. **Identify** non-conformance.
2. **Log in** to NCR System.
3. **Create NCR** (Fill details + Attach Photos).
4. **Submit** for Approval (Finder Manager).
5. **Disposition** assigned (Receiver Manager).
6. **Execute** Action (Rework/Scrap).
7. **Verify & Close** (QC Manager).

## 2. Key Definitions
- **NCR**: Non-Conformance Report (Laporan Ketidaksesuaian).
- **CAPA**: Corrective & Preventive Action (Tindakan Perbaikan & Pencegahan).
- **Finder**: Department that found the issue.
- **Receiver**: Department responsible for the issue.
- **Disposition**: Immediate action taken (Use as-is, Rework, Scrap, Return).

## 3. Status Meaning
- **Draft**: Saved but not submitted.
- **Pending Approval**: Waiting for Manager review.
- **Open**: Approved, pending disposition/action.
- **In Progress (CAPA)**: Action plan is being executed.
- **Pending Verification**: Action done, waiting for QC check.
- **Closed**: Completed and verified.
- **Rejected**: Returned for revision.
- **Void**: Cancelled.

## 4. Tips for Good Reporting
- **Be Specific**: Don't say "Broken". Say "Cracked on left side, 5mm length".
- **Use Photos**: A picture is worth 1000 words.
- **Select Correct Departments**: Ensure the right people get notified.
- **Check Email**: Approvals and assignments come via email.
