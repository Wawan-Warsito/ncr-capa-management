# NCR-CAPA Management System Training

## Slide 1: Welcome & Introduction
- **Title**: Introduction to NCR-CAPA Management System
- **Objective**: Understand the purpose and basic functions of the new system.
- **Presenter**: IT Department / QA Manager
- **Duration**: 10 Minutes

## Slide 2: What is NCR & CAPA?
- **NCR (Non-Conformance Report)**: A document that reports any deviation from standard operating procedures or specifications.
- **CAPA (Corrective & Preventive Action)**:
  - **Corrective Action**: Action to eliminate the cause of a detected non-conformity.
  - **Preventive Action**: Action to eliminate the cause of a potential non-conformity.

## Slide 3: System Overview
- **Web-based Application**: Accessible via browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
- **Key Features**:
  - Digital Reporting & Approval Workflow.
  - Real-time Status Tracking.
  - Automated Notifications (Email).
  - Centralized Database & Analytics.

## Slide 4: User Roles & Responsibilities
- **General User**: Report NCRs, view assigned tasks.
- **Finder Manager**: Approve NCRs raised by their department.
- **Receiver Manager**: Investigate NCRs assigned to their department, assign disposition.
- **QC Manager**: Verify CAPA effectiveness, close NCRs.
- **Admin**: Manage users, master data, and system settings.

## Slide 5: The NCR Workflow (High Level)
1. **Draft**: User creates NCR.
2. **Review**: Finder Manager approves.
3. **Investigation**: Receiver Manager investigates & assigns disposition.
4. **Action**: CAPA created (if needed) & executed by PIC.
5. **Verification**: QC Manager verifies results.
6. **Closed**: Process complete.

## Slide 6: How to Create an NCR (Demo)
- **Step 1**: Login.
- **Step 2**: Click "New NCR".
- **Step 3**: Fill in details (Project, Defect Description, Severity).
- **Step 4**: Upload evidence (Photos).
- **Step 5**: Submit.

## Slide 7: Handling Approvals (For Managers)
- **Notification**: Receive email or check Dashboard.
- **Action**: "Approve" or "Reject" (with comments).
- **Timeline**: Approval expected within 24 hours.

## Slide 8: Managing CAPA
- **Root Cause Analysis (RCA)**: Use 5 Whys or Fishbone (outside system), document in system.
- **Action Plan**: Define steps, timeline, and resources.
- **Progress Updates**: PIC updates % completion regularly.

## Slide 9: Dashboards & Reporting
- **Personal Dashboard**: My Tasks, My NCRs.
- **Department Dashboard**: Department performance metrics.
- **Export**: Download data to Excel/PDF for meetings.
