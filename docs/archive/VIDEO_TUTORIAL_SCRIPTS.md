# NCR-CAPA Management System - Video Tutorial Scripts

## Video 1: System Overview & Login

**Duration**: 2:00
**Audience**: All Users

**Script**:
1. **[0:00 - 0:15]** "Welcome to the NCR-CAPA Management System tutorial. In this video, we will cover how to log in and navigate the main dashboard."
2. **[0:15 - 0:45]** (Screen: Login Page) "Open your web browser and go to the system URL. Enter your email and password provided by the IT department. Click 'Sign In'."
3. **[0:45 - 1:30]** (Screen: Dashboard) "Once logged in, you will see your Dashboard. Here you have a quick overview of your assigned tasks, pending approvals, and recent activity. You can also view system-wide statistics if you have the appropriate permissions."
4. **[1:30 - 2:00]** "Use the left sidebar to navigate to 'My Tasks', 'NCR List', or 'CAPA List'. To log out, click your profile icon in the top right corner and select 'Logout'. That concludes the overview."

---

## Video 2: Creating a New NCR

**Duration**: 3:30
**Audience**: General Users / Initiators

**Script**:
1. **[0:00 - 0:20]** "In this video, we will learn how to create and submit a new Non-Conformance Report (NCR)."
2. **[0:20 - 0:50]** (Screen: NCR List) "Click on 'New NCR' button in the top right corner or the sidebar menu."
3. **[0:50 - 2:00]** (Screen: Create NCR Form) "Fill in the required fields. Select the Project Name and Customer. Identify the Finder Department (your dept) and the Receiver Department (responsible dept). Describe the defect clearly in the 'Defect Description' box. Choose the Defect Category and Severity Level."
4. **[2:00 - 2:45]** "Upload any supporting evidence like photos or documents using the 'Attachments' section. This helps the Receiver Department understand the issue better."
5. **[2:45 - 3:30]** "Review your entries. Click 'Submit' to send the NCR for approval to your Department Manager. You will receive an email notification once it is approved or rejected."

---

## Video 3: Approving NCRs (Manager Role)

**Duration**: 2:30
**Audience**: Department Managers

**Script**:
1. **[0:00 - 0:20]** "This video is for Department Managers. We will show you how to review and approve NCRs submitted by your team."
2. **[0:20 - 1:00]** (Screen: Dashboard / My Approvals) "Navigate to 'My Approvals' from the sidebar. You will see a list of NCRs with status 'Pending Approval'."
3. **[1:00 - 1:45]** (Screen: NCR Detail) "Click on an NCR ID to view details. Review the defect description and attachments. Ensure the information is accurate and complete."
4. **[1:45 - 2:30]** "If everything looks good, click 'Approve'. The NCR will move to the next stage (Receiver Department). If corrections are needed, click 'Reject' and add a comment explaining what needs to be fixed. The initiator will be notified to revise it."

---

## Video 4: Creating and Managing CAPA

**Duration**: 4:00
**Audience**: Receiver Dept / PIC / QA

**Script**:
1. **[0:00 - 0:30]** "This video covers the Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA) process. This is usually initiated by the Receiver Department or QA."
2. **[0:30 - 1:15]** (Screen: NCR Detail - Disposition) "Once an NCR is approved and assigned a disposition (like Rework), you may need to create a CAPA. Click the 'Create CAPA' button on the NCR detail page."
3. **[1:15 - 2:30]** (Screen: Create CAPA Form) "Assign a Person In Charge (PIC). Document the Root Cause Analysis (RCA). Outline the Corrective Action steps and set a Target Date for completion."
4. **[2:30 - 3:15]** (Screen: CAPA Detail) "The assigned PIC can update the progress. Go to the CAPA detail page, click 'Update Progress', enter the percentage completed, and add comments."
5. **[3:15 - 4:00]** "Once the action is 100% complete, mark it as 'Completed'. The QA Manager will then verify the effectiveness and close the CAPA."
